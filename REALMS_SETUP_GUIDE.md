# Shin Sonic on Switch via Realms - Setup Guide

## What You'll Need
- Android phone/tablet with Minecraft Bedrock
- Nintendo Switch with Minecraft Bedrock
- Microsoft/Xbox account (same on both OR friended)
- Realms subscription (~$8/month)

---

## STEP 1: Set Up Android

1. Install **Minecraft** from Google Play Store (if not already)
2. Sign in with your **Microsoft/Xbox account**

---

## STEP 2: Download Shin Sonic Addon

1. Open Chrome on Android
2. Go to: https://mcpedl.com/the-sonic-tapes/
3. Scroll down and tap the download link
4. Download the `.mcaddon` file
5. Open your Downloads folder
6. Tap the `.mcaddon` file
7. Select **"Open with Minecraft"**
8. Wait for "Import successful" message

---

## STEP 3: Create the World

1. Open Minecraft â†’ **Play** â†’ **Create New World**
2. Name it: **"Shin Sonic World"**
3. Scroll to **Add-Ons** section:
   - Tap **Resource Packs** â†’ Find "Shin Sonic" or "Sonic Tapes" â†’ Tap **+**
   - Tap **Behavior Packs** â†’ Find "Shin Sonic" or "Sonic Tapes" â†’ Tap **+**

## STEP 4: Subscribe to Realms

### Option A: Subscribe on Android
1. In Minecraft, tap **Play**
2. Tap **Create New** â†’ **New Realm**
3. Choose **Realms Plus** ($7.99/month) - includes bonus content
   - Or **Realms** ($3.99/month) - basic, 2 players max
4. Complete purchase through Google Play

### Option B: Subscribe on Switch
1. Same steps but purchase through Nintendo eShop

---

## STEP 5: Upload World to Realm

1. On Android, go to **Play**
2. Find your **Realm** (it will have a green icon)
3. Tap the **pencil/edit icon** next to it
4. Tap **Game** â†’ **Replace World**
5. Select **"Shin Sonic World"** from your worlds
6. Confirm upload
7. Wait for upload to complete

---

## STEP 6: Play on Switch!

1. Open Minecraft on Switch
2. Make sure you're signed into the **same Microsoft account** (or friended)
3. Go to **Play** â†’ Look for your Realm (green icon)
4. Tap to join!
5. Shin Sonic will be waiting...

## Troubleshooting

**Addon not working on Realm?**
- Make sure experimental features were ON when creating the world
- Try re-uploading the world

**Can't see Realm on Switch?**
- Verify same Microsoft account on both devices
- Check internet connection
- Restart Minecraft

**Shin Sonic not spawning?**
- He spawns naturally - explore and wait
- Check behavior pack is active
